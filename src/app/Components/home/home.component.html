    • Starters

    Starters, often called appetizers, consist of some interesting varieties of foods that are eaten before the main meal. They usually include delicate and bite-sized morsels of meats or vegetables that can rouse your taste buds as well as your appetite.

    Main Courses

    The traditional Italian meal is structured in 4 parts: antipasti (starters), primo (pasta, that includes dishes such as risotto), secondo (a meat dish, including fish), and dolce (dessert). There is also the optional contorni (sides, generally potatoes, salad, vegetables).
